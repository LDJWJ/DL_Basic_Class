재현율(recall, 민감도, TPR)¶

\begin{equation} \text{TPR} = \frac{\text{TP}}{\text{TP} + \text{FN}} \end{equation}

정밀도¶

\begin{equation} \text{} = \frac{\text{TP}}{\text{TP} + \text{FP}} \end{equation}
